This is an attempt of writing a documentation for BenchIT.

Due to the fact that BenchIT was written by many loosely coupled
and more or less independent groups of people, this documentation
also apears loosely connected at one point or the other. One
little excuse for that may be the fact that this documentation was
written with at least two totally different documentationtools,
namely doxygen and robodoc.
The structur of the docu is more or less similar to the structur
of the BenchIT-source-directory for (hopefully) easier navigation.
